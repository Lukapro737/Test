WE ARE

Innovative video game development studio based in Serbia, passionate about merging the artistry of traditional media with the possibilities of modern technology.

Our mission is to craft captivating and immersive experiences that resonate with players and push the boundaries of storytelling and gameplay.

OUR STORY
Nemanja PETROVIĆ
Art Director / Game Designer

There’s truly no better medium for expressing human creativity than pencil and paper. While other technologies may promise to enhance our creative abilities, nothing quite matches the simplicity and power of these timeless tools.

As we face the challenges of modern technology, which can sometimes overshadow the rich knowledge and creativity built over centuries, it’s important to reconnect with our roots.

By embracing traditional media and thoughtfully integrating it with new advancements, we can preserve our essence and continue to flourish as creators.

GAME IN PRODUCTION
Dominate The Machine

In a world where art is increasingly overshadowed by templates and predefined designs, we’ve chosen to embark on a creative journey with a game crafted mainly on paper. This project celebrates the beauty of hand-drawn artistry and the unique charm that comes from embracing the tactile nature of traditional media combined with modern technology.
